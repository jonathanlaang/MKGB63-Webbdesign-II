About Me

As a dedicated 22-year-old student at Karlstad University, I combine my studies with active participation in student life, serving as the vice-chairman of Linda Carlstad and as a board member of the Karlstad Student Union. My long-standing passion for technology drives me to continuously develop my curiosity, knowledge, and skills, with a focus on applying what I learn to practical projects that I truly enjoy. I strive to be proactive and innovative, viewing every challenge as an opportunity to grow and contribute to something greater. Here, you can explore my journey and gain insight into the projects that reflect my dedication and creativity.

Student life is an exhilarating adventure, offering countless opportunities to connect with new people, immerse yourself in diverse experiences, and create memories that last a lifetime. Being involved in the student community not only enriches your university experience but also cultivates lifelong joy and friendships. It’s a time to live fully, embrace new challenges, and grow both personally and professionally.

My Projects

LostSyncs > is a digital index library specifically designed for the artist Kygo, focusing on documenting and archiving his entire music catalog. The website functions as a modern Wikipedia-style platform where design and user-friendliness are key, making it easy for users to quickly find and access the latest and most reliable information about Kygo's work. With a sleek and intuitive interface, LostSyncs ensures that users always have close access to up-to-date details about the artist's music, from early tracks to the latest releases, while supporting the artist by providing concrete and valuable information.

Linda Carlstad Crosscode > The official Github website for the student association Linda Carlstad in Sweden, Karlstad. The association is under and with support from Karlstad University. Linda Carlstad is an association for students studying computer science, web development, and software system design.
